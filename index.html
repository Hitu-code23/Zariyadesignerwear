FAQ

Questions You're
Probably Thinking

Honest answers to everything you'd want to know before saying yes.

How long will the website actually take? +
For most packages, the full website is ready within 2 weeks. The timeline depends on how quickly we receive brand assets (photos, logos, content) from your side. The faster we get materials, the faster we deliver. I'll always give you a realistic date — not an optimistic one.
What if I don't have professional photos yet? +
No problem. We can use high-quality placeholder imagery during development and swap in your real photos when they're ready. I can also recommend a local photographer in Mauritius who specialises in fashion and product shoots if needed.
Can I update the website myself after it's built? +
Yes — 100%. I'll build with a user-friendly content management system and provide full training so your team can add products, update prices, and manage content independently. You'll never be dependent on me for day-to-day changes.
What about hosting and domain? Are those included? +
Domain and hosting are not included in the package price, but I'll guide you through choosing and setting them up — it's typically Rs 3,000–6,000/year depending on your needs. I'll handle the technical setup so you don't have to worry about anything.
What if I already have a website being built elsewhere? +
I understand — and I'm not here to pressure you. But I'd encourage you to compare: are they building something specifically for a fashion brand, or a generic site? If you'd like a second opinion on what you've been shown so far, I'm happy to review it and give you an honest assessment. No cost.
How many revisions do I get? +
During the dedicated review week (Week 4), revisions are unlimited. You can request as many changes as needed — colour tweaks, text edits, layout adjustments — until you're fully satisfied. After launch, minor changes are covered for 30 days at no charge.
What if I want to add features later — like a loyalty programme or blog? +
The website is built to grow. Adding new features, pages, or integrations after launch is straightforward and will be quoted separately at a fair rate. Many of my clients start with the Professional package and expand once they see results.
